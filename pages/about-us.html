Our Story

SaffCoZ is a famous perfume brand founded in 2010, renowned for its unique and long-lasting perfumes. SaffCoZ aims to create memorable, luxurious fragrances, offering a wide range of variants from sweet and fruity to musky and bold. SaffCoZ has received significant attention for its top-tier quality ingredients, solidifying its reputation in the perfume industry.

SaffCoz was created with a clear vision: to elevate luxury through unforgettable fragrances. Founded by fragrance lovers with a passion for capturing life’s precious moments, our brand transforms memories into exquisite scents. Each fragrance we offer is a unique composition, meticulously crafted with premium ingredients and perfected by skilled perfumers.

Inspired by the beauty of nature and the individuality of each person, our scents are designed to evoke feelings and create lasting impressions.

We meticulously select only the finest ingredients, from sweet floral essences and fresh fruity tones to bold, musky undertones. Every drop of our perfume is crafted to meet the highest standards, ensuring a long-lasting scent with a unique, captivating character.

Signature Scents

With over 25 carefully crafted fragrances, SaffCoz presents a world of luxurious scents designed to make every moment unforgettable. Among them, three signature scents stand out, each a unique blend that captures the essence of sophistication and allure. Discover the allure of our top creations:

Eclipse Noir

A dark, musky fragrance that balances sensual oud with a hint of wild blackcurrant and leather. Bold and captivating, Eclipse Noir is made for those who leave a lasting impression.

Amber Veil

A warm, radiant blend of amber and juicy pear, softened with a whisper of vanilla and cedarwood. This scent wraps you in a golden aura, perfect for those who cherish elegance and warmth.

Velvet Citrus

A refreshing twist of ripe mandarin and bergamot, deepened by earthy musk and a touch of patchouli. Vibrant yet refined, Velvet Citrus is ideal for anyone who loves a fresh, fruity scent with lasting depth.
